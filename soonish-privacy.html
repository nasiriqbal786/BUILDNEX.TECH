Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 26, 2026

At BuildNex.Tech, we believe your data is yours alone. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to your privacy when you use our application, Soonish ("the App").

1. Data Storage & Local Processing

We built Soonish with a strict "Local-First" architecture. All your reminders, tasks, and settings are stored locally on your device using localStorage or local database mechanisms. We do not operate any external database to sync or store your personal data.

2. Information Collection And Use

We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit any Personally Identifiable Information (PII). Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not ask for your name, email address, or phone number within the App.
  • We do not record your keystrokes or the contents of your reminders.

3. Advertising and Analytics

Soonish is completely ad-free. To protect your privacy:

  • We have explicitly disabled Firebase Advertising ID collection.
  • We do not integrate third-party ad networks tracking SDKs into the App.
  • We do not track your behavior across other apps or websites.

4. We Do Not Sell Data

Because we do not gather any data about you, there is absolutely no data for us to sell or share with any third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.

5. Required Permissions

The App may request permissions necessary for core functionality (such as displaying notifications on your lock screen). These permissions are strictly used to operate the features as intended on your device and never to harvest information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us
